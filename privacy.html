Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Meridian ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or contact us through our forms.

Information We Collect

When you fill out a contact form or request an estimate, we collect:

  • Name — so we know how to address you
  • Email address — to send you a response
  • Phone number — to contact you about your project
  • Project details — the information you choose to share in your message
  • Source attribution — if you arrived via a campaign link (e.g., a postcard QR code), we store the UTM campaign/source parameter for tracking purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide the estimate or information you requested
  • To communicate about your project, including scheduling and follow-up
  • To improve our marketing efforts (e.g., understanding which campaigns generate leads)
  • To comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers, who may process your data on our behalf:

  • Formspree / Web3Forms / Netlify Forms — handles form submissions and delivers them to our email
  • Google Analytics / Plausible — website analytics (anonymized where possible)
  • Hosting provider — stores website files (Netlify, Vercel, Cloudflare Pages, or GitHub Pages)

Each provider has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above, or as required by law. If you'd like us to delete your data, contact us and we'll remove it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

Cookies

Our website may use cookies for analytics purposes. We do not use advertising cookies or sell your data to third parties.
